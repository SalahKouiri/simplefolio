DeepMove(Ongoing Training)

DeepMove is an AI-powered chess coaching bot designed to match players at their skill level, analyze their mistakes, and provide tailored exercises for improvement. It actively adapts its playstyle over time, ensuring a dynamic learning experience. Currently, DeepMove is undergoing intensive training in chess strategy using deep learning and reinforcement learning with PyTorch, focusing on improving its ability to evaluate positions, make optimal moves, and refine long-term planning. By continuously playing against itself and learning from games and puzzles sourced from the open database of Lichess, the bot is evolving into a more sophisticated player capable of offering high-level recommendations. As its training progresses, DeepMove will expand into advanced coaching features, making it not just a strong opponent but an AI mentor that improves alongside the user.

Mathio(V1.0)

Mathio is a scientific calculator developed using C programming, employing Shunting Yard Algorithm and Reverse Polish Notation (RPN) for precise expression handling and real-time computation. It currently supports standard arithmetic, trigonometry, logarithms, and basic calculations while actively evolving to integrate graphing capabilities, probability functions, matrix operations, complex number handling, and advanced calculus including derivatives, integrals, and limits. As development progresses, Mathio will expand into a more comprehensive mathematical tool, optimizing performance with multi-threading and memory efficiency, and incorporating AI-driven problem-solving and explanations. By fine-tuning a large language model (LLM), Mathio aims to provide step-by-step solutions, interactive tutoring, and adaptive learning, transforming it into an AI-powered math teacher capable of guiding users through complex concepts in an intuitive way.
